Corn Dogs

Did you know that corn dogs are actually quite easy to make at home?

Well, they are! This homemade corn dog recipe is the perfect treat for when you can't make it to the fair or carnival.

Ingredients

  • Cornmeal and flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt and pepper
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Oil
  • Beef frankfurters

Steps

  1. Make the batter by combining the dry ingredients, then stirring in the milk and egg.
  2. Skewer the frankfurters and roll in the batter.
  3. Fry the corn dogs in hot oil until lightly browned.
  4. Enjoy !
